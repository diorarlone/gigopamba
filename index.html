🦇 How to Use Gigo

Sequencer

  • The sequencer scrolls horizontally — all tracks visible, steps go right.
  • Click the track label (left) to preview that sound.
  • Click any step to toggle. Right-click to cycle velocity.
  • STEPS 16/32/64 to extend pattern.

Piano Tab

  • Full interactive piano keyboard. Click keys to play notes.
  • Use keyboard keys shown on the whites (A–; bottom row, W–P top row).
  • Octave +/− to shift pitch range.

Drum Pads Tab

  • 16 big pads — click to trigger, or use keys 1-8, Q-I.

Recording

  • ⏺ REC BEAT — Records everything, no time limit. Click stop to download.
  • 🎙️ VOICE — Records mic + beat together.

Kicks Button

  • Top-left KICKS button to pick Standard / Hard / Tape kick.
